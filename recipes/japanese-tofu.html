Japanese Tofu Recipe

Description

A recipe for Honey Sesame Seaweed Tofu in the Japanese Style.

Ingredients

  • XX g of Japanese Tofu
  • 4 tsp Oyster Sauce
  • 4 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 sp Sesame Oil
  • 3 soup spoons of Honey
  • 2 spoons Drinking Water
  • XX g of Tapioca Flour
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Dried Seaweed

Steps:

  1. Cut the Japanese Tofu into bite-size pieces.
  2. Air fry the Japanese Tofu pieces until brown (for around 5 minutes at XXX Â°C).
  3. Remove the tofu from the air fryer and set it aside.
  4. Mix the oyster sauce, soy sauce, sesame oil, honey, tapioca flour and drinking water together.
  5. Heat the sauce mixture in a saucepan and stir until it boils. Wait for XX minutes.
  6. Turn off the heat. Then, throw the tofu pieces in the pan and stir until they are fully coated.
  7. Remove the tofu and sauce, and place in a serving dish.
  8. Sprinkle sesame seeds and dried seaweed on the dish.
  9. Enjoy the dish.
