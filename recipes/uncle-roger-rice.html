Uncle Roger's Egg Fried Rice

Description

The correct way to make egg fried rice. No colanders or chilli jam involved. Honed to perfection by Uncle Roger's ancestors over many generations. Now you no need to scare egg fried rice, you only need to scare ghost.

Ingredient (no quantity listed, just use feeling):

  • Garlic
  • Shallot
  • Egg
  • Spring onion
  • Chilli
  • Chicken rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Asian sesame oil
  • Pork lard OR peanut oil (or vegetable oil if you weak)
  • White pepper (optional)
  • and of course... MSG
  • AND NO VEGETABLE. Vegetable taste like sad.

Steps:

  1. Oil the wok with pork lard/peanut oil/vegetable oil
  2. Fry garlic and shallot until fragrant
  3. Add egg, fry until fluffy
  4. Add rice. Stir
  5. Add soy sauce, sesame oil, MSG, white pepper. Stir
  6. Add spring onion, chilli
  7. TOSS
  8. Enjoy your egg fry rice while judging Jamie Oliver
